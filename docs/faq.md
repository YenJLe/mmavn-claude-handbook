Not without checking with the project lead first. Treat Claude like you would a new contractor: verify before sharing.

**Does Claude save my chats?**
Yes, by default, unless you turn on incognito mode or delete them manually.

## Using Claude

**Which model should I pick?**
Sonnet for most work. Opus for hard strategy, long documents, or anything where quality matters more than speed. Haiku for quick throwaway tasks.

**Why is Claude sometimes wrong?**
It is a language model, not a database. It can invent things that sound right. Always verify facts, numbers, and quotes against the source.

**How do I make Claude write in Vietnamese?**
Ask in Vietnamese, or tell it "respond in Vietnamese." For brand-voice work, paste a sample of the target tone first.
