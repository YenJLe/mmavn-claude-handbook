# Prompting Basics

*Coming soon. The handful of prompting habits that actually change the quality of what you get back.*

## Planned content

- **Give context first.** Who is the audience, what is the deliverable, what has been tried.
- **Attach the source.** Upload the brief or paste the original instead of paraphrasing.
- **Be specific about format.** Word count, tone, structure, language.
- **Show an example.** One example of "what good looks like" beats a paragraph of adjectives.
- **Iterate, do not rewrite.** Ask Claude to adjust the previous answer. Don't start over.
- **Use the right model.** Sonnet for everyday work, Opus for hard problems, Haiku for quick tasks.
